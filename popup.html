OSM iD: Custom Layer Settings

This extension allows you to add custom background map layers to the OpenStreetMap iD editor. Define your preferred map layers below with a name and URL template.
Once saved, open the iD editor, click on the "Background Settings" button (layers icon), then on "Edit custom background" (three dots icon).
Your custom layers will appear as buttons in the new pop-up panel. Click a button to instantly apply your desired background!
